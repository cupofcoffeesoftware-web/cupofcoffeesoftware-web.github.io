Overview

Apples of Gold is a simple reading tracker. It does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything the app stores lives entirely on your device.

What We Collect

Nothing. Apples of Gold does not collect any personal information. There are no accounts, no sign-ins, no analytics, and no servers.

What Stays on Your Device

The following data is stored locally in your device's app storage and never leaves your device:

  • Your reading progress (which days you've completed and when)
  • Your streak history
  • Your reading plan selections and custom plans
  • Your notification reminder settings (time of day)

This data is yours. It is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, Apples of Gold uses your device's local notification system to deliver them. These notifications are generated entirely on your device — no data is sent to any external server.

Third-Party Services

Apples of Gold does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data services.

Children's Privacy

Apples of Gold does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a future version of the app, the updated policy will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date.
